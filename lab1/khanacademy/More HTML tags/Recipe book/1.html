Recipe #1

  • Time: 30–40 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Snapper fish 1 whole (700–800 g)
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Lemon 1
Garlic 2 cloves
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste
Paprika / chili (optional) ½ tsp
Fresh herbs (parsley, thyme) A little

Step 1: Clean and wash the snapper, then pat it dry with paper towels.

Step 2: In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, and paprika.

Step 3: Rub the marinade all over the fish and let it sit for 15–20 minutes.

Step 4: Preheat the grill. Cook the snapper for 6–8 minutes on each side until the flesh is cooked through and flakes easily.

Step 5: Garnish with lemon slices and fresh herbs, and serve hot.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Burger buns 2
Ground beef 300 g
Cheese slices 2
Lettuce 2 leaves
Tomato 1
Onion ½
Salt to taste
Black pepper to taste
Cooking oil 1 tbsp

Step 1: Put the ground beef in a bowl, add salt and black pepper, and mix well.

Step 2: Form the meat into two burger patties.

Step 3: Heat oil in a pan and cook the patties for 4–5 minutes on each side.

Step 4: Place cheese on the patties and let it melt.

Step 5: Assemble the burger with buns, meat, lettuce, tomato, and onion.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 1 hour 30 minutes (including marinating)
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken or lamb cubes 500 g
Onion 1 large
Bell pepper 1
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Lemon juice 2 tbsp
Garlic 2 cloves
Salt to taste
Black pepper to taste
Paprika 1 tsp
Wooden skewers 4-6

Step 1: Cut the meat into bite-sized cubes.

Step 2: Mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, and paprika in a bowl.

Step 3: Marinate the meat in the mixture for 1 hour in the fridge.

Step 4: Cut onion and bell pepper into chunks.

Step 5: Thread meat and vegetables alternately onto the skewers.

Step 6: Grill or barbecue the skewers for 10–15 minutes, turning occasionally until cooked.
